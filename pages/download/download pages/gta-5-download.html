Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5), developed by Rockstar Games, is an open-world action-adventure game that has captivated millions of players worldwide since its release in 2013. Set in the sprawling, fictional city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas, the game offers a gripping narrative, immersive gameplay, and unparalleled freedom to explore a richly detailed world.
